Government Support for Farmers

Available Schemes

  • → PM Kisan Samman Nidhi: Direct income support for farmers.

    • What is PM-Kisan Yojana? Pradhan Mantri Kisan Samman Nidhi (PM-KISAN) is a Central Sector Scheme launched by the Government of India in February 2019.
    It aims to provide financial support to all landholding farmer families in the country.


  • → Soil Health Card Scheme: Provides soil fertility details.

    • What is the Soil Health Card Scheme? The Soil Health Card (SHC) Scheme was launched by the Government of India on 19th February 2015 to promote soil testing and balanced use of fertilizers based on the soil’s health condition. The goal is to enhance farm productivity and ensure sustainable farming.

  • → Crop insurance scheme.

    • What is PMFBY? Pradhan Mantri Fasal Bima Yojana (PMFBY) is a crop insurance scheme launched by the Government of India on 18th February 2016. Its objective is to provide financial support to farmers in case of crop failure due to natural calamities, pests, and diseases.
