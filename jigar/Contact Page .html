→ National Helpline for PM-KISAN:

   The official PM-KISAN helpline number is 155261 (toll-free) for resolving any registration or payment-related issues.

→ State Agriculture Helplines:

   Every state has a dedicated helpline for agricultural queries (e.g., Toll-Free numbers like 1800-180-1551 for agricultural-related queries).

→ Kisan Call Centers (KCC):

Farmers can dial 1551 to get information about government schemes, crop diseases, and other agriculture-related issues.
